    Frequently Asked Questions

    Account and Session​

    How long does my session stay active?

    Your session remains active for 7 days from the last login. After that period, or if you manually sign out, you'll need to enter your DNI and password again.

    Can I use the same account on multiple phones?

    The account is personal, but there is no technical restriction preventing you from using it on more than one device. However, sessions are not synchronized between devices: if you sign out on one, the other will remain active until it expires.

    Can I change my DNI?

    Not from the app. The DNI is the unique identifier for your account and cannot be modified within the application itself. If you need to change it, contact the gym administrator.

    What happens if I enter the wrong password several times?

    There is no lockout for failed attempts in the current version. If you don't remember your password, use the "Forgot your password?" feature to reset it.

    Machines and Bookings​

    Can I book multiple machines at the same time?

    Yes, but not in overlapping time slots. You cannot have two simultaneous bookings even on different machines. You can have, for example, a booking at 9:00 and another at 10:00.

    How long can I book a machine for?

    The maximum limit per booking is 1 hour. The default duration set by the app is 30 minutes, but you can adjust it before confirming.

    How far in advance can I make a booking?

    There is no advance limit. You can book for today or for several days ahead, as long as the machine is available at that time.

    What happens if a machine goes into maintenance and I had a booking?

    Your booking is automatically canceled. The next time you open the app you'll see a notice with the machine name and the affected booking. You can make a new booking on another available machine.

    Can I modify an existing booking (change the date or time)?

    There is no direct edit function for bookings. To change a booking's details you must cancel it and create a new one with the correct date and time.

    Notifications​

    Why am I not receiving reminders for my bookings?

    The most common causes are:

    1. You didn't grant the app permission to show notifications. Check it in Phone Settings → Apps → Trainium → Notifications.
    2. The phone was off or in airplane mode at the time of the alert.
    3. The configured lead time has already passed: if you set 10 minutes and more than 10 minutes have passed since you made the booking, the alert won't be sent.

    Can I receive reminders if the app is closed?

    Yes. Reminders work even when the app is closed in the background, as they use Android's alarm system.

    Dishes and Nutrition​

    Can I suggest dishes without being a Premium user?

    Yes. The dish suggestion feature is available to all registered users, regardless of their account type.

    How long does it take for a suggestion to be approved?

    It depends on the gym administrator. There is no set timeframe. If you want to know the status of your suggestion, ask the administrator directly.

    Why can't I see the suggestion I submitted?

    Suggestions do not appear in the catalog until the administrator approves them. While pending review, they are only visible to the administrator.

    Weight and Tracking​

    Is my weight data private?

    Yes. Only you can see your weight history. No other user or administrator has access to your personal records.

    Why does my weight vary so much from one day to another?

    Body weight can fluctuate between 0.5 and 2 kg from one day to the next completely normally, due to factors like hydration, salt intake, water retention, or menstrual cycle timing. The app's chart helps you see the real long-term trend, which is what matters.

    Can I log my weight more than once a day?

    No. The app only allows one entry per day. If you made a mistake in the entered value, you can edit it from the log list.

    Premium and Payments​

    Does the subscription renew automatically?

    No. The subscription does not renew automatically. When it expires you must manually purchase a new plan.

    How do I know when my subscription expires?

    On the main screen you'll see the days remaining next to your name. When time is almost up, the indicator will change to "Expires today" on the last day of the subscription.

    Can I request a refund if I cancel the subscription before it expires?

    The app does not handle automatic refunds. If you have a claim of this type, contact the gym administrator directly.

    Connection and Data​

    Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

    Yes. All information (bookings, dishes, weight, profile) is stored in the cloud. Without an internet connection it is not possible to perform any operation. If you lose connection while using the app, you'll see an error message and a retry button.

    Does the app save data on my phone?

    The app only saves your session locally (so you don't have to log in every time) and your theme, language, and notification preferences. All other data (bookings, weight, profile) is in the cloud.
